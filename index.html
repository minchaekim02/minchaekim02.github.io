Minchae Kim

Human-AI Interaction Researcher

I am a first-year Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Bongwon Suh at the Human Centered Computing Lab (HCCL).

My research focuses on Human–AI Interaction, with a strong interest in understanding people deeply and designing AI technologies that genuinely support human well-being.

I believe technology, at its best, can help build a healthier and happier world for everyone—and toward that future I want my research to point.
