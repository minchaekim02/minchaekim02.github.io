Human-AI Interaction Researcher

Integrated M.S./Ph.D. Student · Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence
Seoul National University  ·  Human Centered Computing Lab
Advisor: Prof. Bongwon Suh

I am a first-year Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Bongwon Suh at the Human Centered Computing Lab (HCCL).

My research focuses on Human-AI Interaction, with a particular emphasis on AI companion systems. I am interested in understanding how people form and sustain relationships with AI companions, and how we can design these systems to foster meaningful, healthy, and emotionally safe interactions.

My work is informed by theories from psychology and social science—such as Social Penetration Theory and cognitive reframing—and bridges behavioral science with HCI methodology. I aim to design AI companions that are not only engaging but also relationally responsible.

Research Interests
