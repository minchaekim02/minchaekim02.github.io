Minchae Kim

Human-AI Interaction Researcher

I am a first-year Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Bongwon Suh in the Human Centered Computing Lab (HCCL).

My research focuses on Human–AI Interaction, driven by a strong interest in understanding people deeply and designing AI technologies that genuinely support human well-being.

I believe that, at its best, technology can help build a healthier and happier world for everyone—and that's the future I want my research to point toward.
